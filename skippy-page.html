Facts about Skippy:

  • His full name is Amore Skipper
  • He is a Havanese
  • He weighs about 12 pounds
  • His breed originally came from Cuba.
  • More info about Havanese can be found here

What Skippy does for fun:

  1. Skippy sleeps in his bed.
  2. Skippy plays fetch.
  3. Skippy pulls his toys apart.
  4. Skippy chews his paws.

Tricks that Skippy can do:

  • Sit
  • Lie Down
  • Go to bed

Additional Information:

Skippy is a very dumb dog. He can also be very hyper. He likes to hang around other people and sit in their lap. If he gets too excited, though, he will leak (he is a leaky dog).

He will normally spend his days alternating between sleeping on the couch, sleeping on someone's lap, sleeping in his bed, and playing with his toys. He likes to play games, but he is pretty bad when it comes to tricks.
