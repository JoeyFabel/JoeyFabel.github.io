Facts about Ace:

  • His full name is Aesop
  • He is a Coton De Tulear
  • He weighs about 20 pounds
  • His breed is the official dog of Madagascar
  • More info about Cotons can be found here

What Ace does for fun:

  1. Ace eats his food.
  2. Ace sleeps on the couch.
  3. Ace eats Skippy's food.
  4. Ace sleeps in his bed

Tricks that Ace can do:

  • Beg
  • Lie down
  • Roll Over
  • Play Dead

Additional Information:

Ace is a very cute dog. He is also very social. He likes to hang around other people and sit by their feet. If you are lucky, he might even sit by your lap.

He will normally spend his days alternating between sleeping on the couch, sleeping on the ground, sleeping in his bed, and resting in the sun outside. He does not like to play games as much as Skippy, but he is better than him at tricks.
